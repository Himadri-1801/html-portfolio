Himadri's all-time top 3

This list contains my favorite movies ,anime and webseries .

Breaking Bad

This series is about the journey of Walter White from being a simple high-school chemistry teacher to becoming one of the county's most wanted druglords.

Attack on Titan

This anime is set up in a post-apocalyptic world where most of humanity was destroyed by huge man-eating titans. Our protagonists try to solve the mystery behind the sudden appearance of these creatures while trying to find a way to save the rest of humanity.

Death Note

Unlike other animes this one presents an ani-hero and deals with a controversial topic regarding death penalty through some supernatural elements.
